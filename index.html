FAQs

▼ What is HealthyRx™?
HealthyRx™ is a membership program that provides its members with access to generic prescription drugs for free and brand name drugs for a discount.
▼Is HealthyRx™ a prescription drug program?
HealthyRx™ is not: (1) an insurance plan; (2) a Qualified Health Plan under the Affordable Care Act; or (3) a Medicare Part D prescription drug plan.
▼Is HealthyRx™ medical insurance?
HealthyRx™ does not meet the minimum creditable coverage requirements or replace COBRA or any other medical insurance program.
▼Are all drugs free?
YES! All generic drugs are completely free, including shipping to your house. If a generic version of a drug is not available, a brand-name drug is likely available at a significant discount.
▼Are there any limits on the drugs I can get?
You will typically receive a 90-day supply of your prescription. If the quantity/dose of your prescription changes, you may incur a small additional fee for exceeding free shipping allowances. Controlled substances are not available.
▼What if I have questions about my prescription?
Connect with your HealthyRx™ pharmacist for assistance 24-hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year. Pharmacy staff are available through web chat, support in the application, or phone
▼Can I use my HSA/FSA to make any necessary payments?
Prescriptions and delivery are FREE! If an employer requires that employees contribute to their HealthyRx™ membership, employees may be able to obtain reimbursement from their HSA/FSA.
▼Is there any exclusion for pre-existing conditions?
There are no exclusions for pre-existing conditions.
▼Is there a waiting period?
Benefits may start immediately upon enrollment or the first day of the month. If you choose to start on the day you enroll, you will pay for that month’s membership fee in full regardless of the number of days remaining in the month.
▼Are there long-term contracts?
Individuals and employees are not locked into any long-term contract. Membership benefits are renewed monthly at the employer’s discretion. Employers may cancel at any time with no penalties.
▼Are there refunds?
Each HealthyRx™ membership is paid monthly in advance. If an employer cancels prior to the start of the month, they will receive a refund for that month. Once the month starts, the membership fees for that month are non-refundable.
▼How are membership fees paid?
Membership fees are paid in advance of the upcoming membership month via automatic ACH deduction or credit card payment.
▼Can I prepay for a year of membership?
Annual plans are available and may include additional benefits. Prepaid plans are non-refundable. Employees added to a prepaid plan will be prorated for the remainder of the year. Employers of pre-paid plans may shift the benefits from a discharged employee to a newly hired employee.
▼Does HealthyRx™ offer any guarantees?
Our goal is your satisfaction. If you are dissatisfied with any service or product we offer, let us know so we can make it right for you.
